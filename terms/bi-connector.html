BI Connector

Software component that integrates RTLS data with Business Intelligence platforms for advanced analytics and reporting. BI connectors typically extract location data from RTLS databases and transform it into dimensional models suitable for BI tools like Tableau, Power BI, QlikView, or SAP BusinessObjects.

Common data transformations include: aggregating position points into zone dwell times, calculating movement paths and distances, joining location data with ERP/MES data (orders, materials, personnel), and computing operational KPIs (cycle times, utilization, throughput). BI integration enables: executive dashboards with high-level metrics, ad-hoc analysis by business users, correlation of location data with quality or performance metrics, and predictive analytics for capacity planning. Update frequencies typically range from near-real-time (1-5 minute refresh) to daily batch loads depending on use case. Well-designed BI connectors provide pre-built data models and report templates for common RTLS analytics scenarios.
