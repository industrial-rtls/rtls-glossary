The application of Industry 4.0 principles to logistics and supply chain operations. RTLS is a key enabling technology providing real-time visibility of goods, vehicles, and equipment throughout logistics networks. Enables automation, optimization, and integration of logistics processes. Supports goals of transparency, efficiency, and responsiveness.

Logistics 4.0

Evolution of logistics and supply chain management incorporating digital technologies and automation aligned with Industry 4.0 principles. Logistics 4.0 encompasses: IoT and RTLS (real-time visibility of materials and assets), autonomous vehicles (AGVs, drones), warehouse automation (AS/RS, robotic picking), predictive analytics (demand forecasting, route optimization), blockchain (supply chain transparency), and AI (intelligent decision-making). RTLS is foundational Logistics 4.0 technology enabling: (1) Real-time inventory visibility - knowing exact location and status of all materials throughout supply chain. (2) Automated tracking - eliminating manual scanning and data entry through continuous position updates. (3) Process automation - triggering actions based on location (automatic putaway, pick confirmation, shipping verification). (4) Dynamic optimization - adjusting operations in real-time based on actual material positions and movements. (5) Integration - connecting physical logistics with digital systems (WMS, TMS, ERP). Logistics 4.0 use cases enabled by RTLS: smart warehouses (material tracking from receiving through storage to picking and shipping), cross-docking operations (automatically routing materials directly from receiving to shipping), returnable container management (tracking pallets, totes, and packaging throughout supply chain), yard management (visibility of trailers and containers in yards), and last-mile delivery (tracking parcels to customer locations). Logistics 4.0 benefits: 20-40% inventory reduction (better visibility enabling lower safety stocks), 30-50% labor efficiency improvement (automation and optimization reducing manual effort), 25-40% space utilization increase (optimized storage and layouts), 40-60% faster order fulfillment (streamlined processes), and 70-90% elimination of shipping errors (automated verification). RTLS investment typically $100-500 per thousand square feet depending on accuracy requirements and integration scope.
